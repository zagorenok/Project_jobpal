Always on hand to ensure you never miss a candidate

Your chatbot will be available 24/7 and respond to questions automatically using our FAQ engine, leaving you with more time to focus on pressing tasks. In the instance that a question posed is too tough for the chatbot, this will then be passed on to a recruiter to follow-up in person.

Putting you infront of the best candidates, quicker

Your chatbot automates tedious and time-consuming jobs such as screening applicants’ CVs, recommending the best of the bunch and scheduling interview appointments. This allows you to cut through the noise and get face-to-face with relevant candidates much sooner than using traditional methods.
